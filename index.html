Our Mission

Rooted in Christ's Boundless Love

Agape Love of Renton reaches out to the unhoused and those battling addiction, offering prayer, nourishing meals, and compassionate support. We don't just meet immediate needs. We ignite hope and walk alongside our neighbors toward lasting change.

Faith · Compassion · Dignity · Community · Hope
Our Heart

Every person deserves
to be seen, fed, and loved.

Agape love means love without condition — the kind that doesn't ask where you've been before it sets a place for you. That's the love we try to carry into every Monday morning, one plate and one prayer at a time.

We serve our neighbors who are unhoused and those in addiction recovery, not because they've earned it, but because everyone deserves dignity, nourishment, and a community that won't give up on them.

"Love your neighbor as yourself." — Mark 12:31

Executive Director

Meet Jeanine Burnley.

Jeanine built Agape Love of Renton on one unshakeable belief: every person deserves to be seen, fed, and loved, regardless of where they are in their journey.

Every Monday morning, she leads her team to Luther's Table in Downtown Renton, serving hot meals, offering prayer, and pouring out a love that never gives up on anyone. Her volunteers are the backbone of everything Agape Love of Renton does, showing up week after week to serve their neighbors with compassion and dedication.
